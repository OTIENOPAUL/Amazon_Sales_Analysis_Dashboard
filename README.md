# 📊 Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)

![Dashboard](Dashboard.png)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI, designed to uncover key business insights across sales performance, customer behavior, product categories, and regional trends.

The goal is not just visualization—but decision support: helping stakeholders understand what is happening, why it’s happening, and where to act.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives
* **Monitor overall sales performance** (Revenue, Profit, Orders)
* **Identify top-performing product categories**
* **Analyze regional sales distribution**
* **Evaluate payment method preferences**
* **Track revenue trends over time**
* **Understand the relationship between discounts and profitability**

## 📊 Dashboard Breakdown

### 🔹 Key Metrics (Top KPIs)
* **Total Orders:** 25K
* **Average Rating:** 3.00
* **Total Revenue:** 16.48M
* **Total Profit:** ~823K

> 👉 These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall business health.

### 🔹 Category Performance
Compares Total Revenue vs Total Profit across product categories:
* Beauty
* Books
* Electronics
* Home & Kitchen
* Fashion
* Sports

> 📌 **Insight:** Some categories generate high revenue but relatively lower profit—indicating possible margin inefficiencies.

### 🔹 Regional Sales Analysis
Sales distribution across:
* Asia
* Europe
* Middle East
* North America

> 📌 **Insight:** Sales are relatively balanced, but slight variations can inform regional marketing strategies.

### 🔹 Payment Method Distribution
Breakdown of customer payment preferences:
* Wallet
* UPI
* Cash on Delivery
* Debit Card
* Credit Card

> 📌 **Insight:** Digital payments dominate, indicating a shift toward cashless transactions.

### 🔹 Revenue Trend (Time Series)
Tracks monthly revenue performance.

> 📌 **Insight:** Noticeable fluctuations across months; peaks suggest seasonal demand patterns.

### 🔹 Discount vs Profit Analysis
Scatter plot showing the relationship between Discounts, Profit, and Product categories.

> 📌 **Insight:** Higher discounts do not always translate to higher profit—critical for pricing strategy.
